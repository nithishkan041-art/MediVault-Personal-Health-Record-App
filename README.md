# 🏥 MediVault – Personal Health Record App

## Project Overview

MediVault is an AI-powered mobile application designed to securely manage personal and family health records in one centralized platform. The app allows users to store medical reports, prescriptions, vaccination records, laboratory test results, and emergency medical information digitally. Instead of carrying physical documents, users can easily access their health records anytime and anywhere. The application integrates Optical Character Recognition (OCR) technology to scan and automatically extract important information from medical documents, making record management faster, more organized, and efficient. MediVault is designed with a modern, clean, and user-friendly interface that provides a seamless experience while maintaining data privacy and security.

## Objective

The primary objective of MediVault is to simplify healthcare record management by providing a secure digital platform for storing and accessing medical information. The app also helps users track medications, schedule doctor appointments, receive vaccination reminders, and maintain complete family health records.

## Design Approach

The application follows a modern and minimal UI/UX design with intuitive navigation, card-based layouts, rounded components, and consistent spacing. The design focuses on accessibility, simplicity, and a smooth user experience suitable for users of all age groups.
